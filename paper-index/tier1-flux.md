### 🔴 MISSING 1: The Purple Pincher Shell Model (CRITICAL)

### 🔴 MISSING 2: Tide Pools as Knowledge Evolution Metaphor (CRITICAL)
**What it is:** A paper that makes the tide pool metaphor as rigorous as the dojo metaphor. Tide pools are where knowledge concentrates and evolves under pressure — the constrained environment forces adaptation, the mixing of ideas from different sources creates novelty, the edges are where the most interesting life happens. This should explain why Purple Pincher uses tidal zones as its knowledge evolution framework.

**Core claim:** The intertidal zone (extreme pressure, mixed environments, high diversity) is the best metaphor for where fleet intelligence emerges. Too calm (deep water) and nothing evolves. Too harsh (supratidal) and nothing survives.

**Why it needs to exist:** Tide pools appear throughout the fleet vocabulary but no paper explains them with the same rigor as the dojo has.
